ATS Overview

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System, Getboarded editor can be implemented directly on your site to receive job applications. All you need to do is put a small button to your application and then send as much info you want as a query string to begin with the profile. Better yet, just upload a resume and let the platform take care of parsing it into right fields.

Getting Started Resources

  • You can choose size of the editor to implement, the editor works in website mode, tablet mode and mobile mode seamlessly.
  • User email chosen will provide you with access to resume created through the implemented editor when you login to Getboarded.com
  • You can order a custom design and have your own resume created from within your platform
